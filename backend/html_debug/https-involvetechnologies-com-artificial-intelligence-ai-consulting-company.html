    Frequently Asked Questions
    What roles can you provide through staff augmentation?

    We specialize in augmenting teams with AI Developers, Blockchain Developers, and Cybersecurity Experts. All our professionals are pre-vetted, technically sound, and ready to integrate into your project with minimal onboarding time.

    How do you ensure the quality of the augmented resources?

    Each resource undergoes a multi-step vetting process, including technical assessments, soft skill evaluations, and domain-specific simulations. We also offer trial periods and replacement guarantees to ensure a perfect fit.

    Are your resources available in different time zones?

    Yes, we offer time-zone-aligned professionals. Whether you’re based in North America, Europe, or APAC, we ensure overlapping working hours to maximize collaboration and productivity.

    Can we scale the team up or down based on project needs?

    Absolutely. We offer flexible engagement models allowing you to scale your team up or down with minimal notice, depending on the project lifecycle and budget.

    What about data security and IP protection?

    We sign NDAs with both clients and team members. Our work environments are SOC2 and ISO 27001 compliant, and we ensure complete IP transfer to the client upon project completion.

    Do you provide resource replacements if needed?

    Yes, if a resource does not meet performance expectations or becomes unavailable, we provide a free replacement within 7–14 business days, as per the SLA.

    Can we interview and select the professionals ourselves?

    Yes, clients have full control over selection. After we shortlist suitable candidates, you can conduct interviews and choose the ones that best fit your team culture and technical needs.

    What kind of ongoing support do you offer post-deployment?

    We offer continuous support including weekly reporting, performance monitoring, bug fixes, and if needed, 24/7 support for security or mission-critical systems.
